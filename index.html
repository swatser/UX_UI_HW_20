BudgIt

A comprehensive money management app for young adults.

Many young adults struggle with taking control of their finances. Despite many recently finishing school, these individuals are not equipped with the right tools for the financial responsibilities that are expected of them, which causes them stress. BudgIt is an app that connects all aspects of an individual’s finances into one intuitive interface. In addition, it creates a judgment-free space for adults to develop their financial literacy.

Research

We focused our research on three objectives:

  • Understand the current level of financial education of the average young adult
  • Discover the tools and methods that young adults currently use to manage their finances
  • Learn what is most frustrating to young adults about managining their own finances

Problem Statement

The shortage of concise information on money management in the education system has caused young adults to lack the confidence or know-how to begin building better financial health for themselves. We have observed that young adults don’t have the skills for financial literacy, which is causing poor financial health and planning for long-term goals.
